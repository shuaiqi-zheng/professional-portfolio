Evolution of Artificial Intelligence Timeline

1950

Turing Test

Alan Turing proposes the imitation game as a test for machine intelligence.

1956

Dartmouth Conference

The field of artificial intelligence is formally introduced.

1974-1980

First AI Winter

Funding declines as expectations exceed computing capabilities.

1997

IBM Deep Blue

Deep Blue defeats chess champion Garry Kasparov.

2012

AlexNet Breakthrough

Deep learning dramatically improves image recognition accuracy.

2016

AlphaGo

DeepMind defeats world champion Lee Sedol in Go.

2022

ChatGPT

Large language models bring generative AI into mainstream use.
